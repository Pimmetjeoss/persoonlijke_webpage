## Design Principles

- **Bold typography**: text-7xl to text-9xl for headings
- **Green monochrome**: 11-shade green palette
- **Interactive elements**: hover color fills, blur-in animations, scramble text effects
- **3px black borders**: used on cards and section dividers
- **White cards on light green backgrounds**: primary content pattern
- **Rounded corners**: rounded-xl consistently
